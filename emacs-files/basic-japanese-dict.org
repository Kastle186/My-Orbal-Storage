* Japanese/English Basic Words and Expressions

** あ

- あげる (上げる): To raise, elevate, launch, give to someone, surface from beneath
- あいだ (間): Space between, gap, interval, duration, period of time
- あいて (相手): Companion, partner, company, opponent, other party
- あく (開く): To open, be empty, be vacant, be open, have an interval
- あるく (歩く): To walk
- あし (足): Foot, paw, leg
- あたま (頭): Head, mind, intellect, tip, top structural component

** ぼ

- ぼく (僕): I, me, you (when addressing a young boy)

** ち

- ちから (力): Power, force, strength, might, capability

** だ

- だいいち (第一): First, foremost, most important, primary, number one in importance
- だれ (誰): Who
- だす (出す): To take out, put out, reveal, submit, send

** で

- でる (出る): To exit, leave, go out, depart, emerge

** ど

- ど (度): Degree (angle, temperature, scale), counter of occurrences, strength of glasses, alcohol volume

** ご

- ご (五): Five, 5

** は

- はいる (入る): To enter, go into, join
- はなし (話): Talk, speech, chat, conversation, discussion

** ひ

- ひかり (光): Light, illumination, ray, gleam, glow
- ひかる (光る): To shine, glitter, be bright
- ひと (人): Person, someone, human

** へ

- へや (部屋): Room, chamber, apartment, flat, pad

** い

- いえ/うち (家): House, residence, home, household, lineage
- いじょう (以上): Not less than, more than, further than, and over, and above
- いれる (入れる): To put in, let in, bring in, install, employ
- いう (言う/云う): To say, utter, name, declare, make a noise

** じ

- じ/とき (時): Time, hour, moment
- じぶん (自分): Myself, oneself

** じゅ

- じゅう (十): Ten, 10, book containing collection of poems

** か

- かんがえる (考える): To think over, ponder, consider, reflect on, bear in mind
- かんじる (感じる): To feel, sense, experience
- かのじょ (彼女): She, her, girlfriend
- かお (顔): Face, visage, expression
- からだ ([身]体): Body, torso, constitution of the body
- かれ (彼): He, him, boyfriend
- かぜ (風): Wind, breeze, draft
- かず/すう (数): Number, amount, quantity

** き

- き (気): Spirit, mind, will, mood, ambience
- きく (聞く): To hear, listen, ask, overhear, eavesdrop
- きみ (君): You, buddy, pal

** こ

- こども (子供): Child
- こえ (声): Voice, singing of a bird, sound, voiced sound, chirping of a critter
- こころ (心): Heart, spirit, meaning of a phrase/riddle/etc
- こし/コシ (腰): Lower back, waist, hips, lumbar region, resilience
- こたえる (答える): To answer, reply
- こと/じ (事): Thing, matter, alias
- ことば (言葉): Word, expression, language

** く

- くび/クビ (首): Neck, dismissal/firing from a job
- くち (口): Mouth, opening, hole, gap, orifice
- くに (国): Country, state, region, province of Japan, land

** ま

- まなこ (眼): Eyeball
- まつ (待つ): To wait, await, look forward to

** め

- め (目): Eye, eyeball, eyesight, vision, eye of a storm/needle/etc

** み

- み (身): Body, oneself, main part
- みえる (見える): To be seen, be in sight, seem
- みる (見る): To see, look, view, observe, examine

** も

- もどる (戻る): To turn back, return, rebound, spring back, be returned
- もくてき (目的): Purpose, goal, aim, objective, intention
- もつ (持つ): To hold in one's hand, carry, have, own, possess

** む

- むね/むな (胸): Chest, breast, bust, feelings, heart

** ね

- ねん/とし (年): Year, age

** に

- にほん (日本): Japan
- にんげん (人間): Human being, mankind, person

** お

- おもう (思う): To think, consider, believe, imagine, expect
- おんな/じょ (女): Woman, female, gal
- おなじ (同じ): Same, identical, equal, equivalent, alike
- おお/だい (大): Large, big, great, major, ultimate
- おれ (俺): I, me
- おと (音): Sound, noise, note (musical)
- おとこ/だん (男): Man, male, fellow, guy, manliness

** ろ

- ろく/むう (六/陸): Six, 6

** せ

- せかい (世界): The world, universe, society

** し

- しかし (): However, but
- しぬ (死ぬ): To die, pass away
- しる (知): To know, be aware of, understand, comprehend, realize

** しょ

- しょうじょ/おとめ (少女): Maiden, young lady, little girl

** す

- すがた (姿): Figure, form, shape, appearance, guise
- すこし (少し): Small quantity, little, few

** た

- たつ (立つ): To stand up, rise, find oneself in a situation

** て

- て (手): Hand, forepaw, hand of cards, handle, help
- てき (敵): Rival, adversary, menace, threat, enemy

** と

- とる (取る): To take, pick up, grab, obtain, keep

** わ

- わらう (笑う): To laugh, amuse
- わるい (悪い): Bad, undesirable, evil, poor quality, sinful
- わたし (私): I, me

** よ

- よぶ (呼ぶ): To call out to, invite, summon
- よん (四): Four, 4
- よる (夜): Evening, night, dinner time
